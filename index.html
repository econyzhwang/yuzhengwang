Yuzheng Wang (王煜正)

I am a PhD candidate at School of Insurance and China Institute for Actuarial Science, Central University of Finance and Economics.

I am a visiting PhD student at Division of the Social Sciences, the University of Chicago.

My research interests span the fields of political economy, economic history, and insurance.

My recent research project focuses on the origins of financial organizations in China.

Hobby

Yuzheng Wang studied violin with his mother since childhood. At the age of ten, he studied violin with Prof. Ding Zhinuo, of the Shanghai Conservatory of Music, for a total of twelve years. He has participated in the Shanghai Conservatory of Music's extracurricular string orchestra for elementary school students and children's choir, and was once the principal violin of the Central University of Finance and Economics Symphony Orchestra. He is now a member of the University of Chicago Chamber Orchestra. Currently, he is interested in Baroque violin performance.
