Research

My research focuses on the origins of financial organizations in China, exploring their historical development and implications for modern institutions.

  • Research Area 1: Political economy of risk management.
  • Research Area 2: Economic history and state capacity in disaster management.
  • Research Area 3: Insurance systems and intergenerational equity.
